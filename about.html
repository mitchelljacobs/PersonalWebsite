ABOUT

Mitchell Jacobs is a writer and comics artist from Minnetonka, Minnesota. While earning an MFA in Creative Writing at Purdue University, he served as managing editor for Sycamore Review. Currently, he lives in Vientiane, teaching English at the National University of Laos. His work appears in journals such as Gulf Coast, Missouri Review, New Ohio Review, Ninth Letter, and Ploughshares.
