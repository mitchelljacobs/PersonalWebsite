ABOUT

Mitchell Jacobs is a writer and comics artist from Minnesota. A graduate of Dartmouth College, he currently serves as managing editor for Sycamore Review. His work has appeared in journals such as Gulf Coast, Lumina, New Ohio Review, Pinball, and Tar River Poetry.
